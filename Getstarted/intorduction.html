Introduction
Techno-UI is an open source styling framework, which helps Developers to use best styles and components, by using pre-defined classnames. Techno-UI helps you to quickly build your Dream Project, by focusing much on Functionality, because we take care of your Designs.
Installation Guide
In order to introduce Techno-UI to your project, Copy the Link given below and paste it in the <head> tag of your html, above all other style sheets. 
                    
        <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://techno-ui.netlify.app/css/components.css">
